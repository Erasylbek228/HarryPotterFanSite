About

Harry Potter is a young wizard famous for surviving an attack by the dark wizard Voldemort as a baby. He grows up in an unkind household with abusive relatives until he discovers his magical heritage and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Harry is characterized by his lightning bolt scar, a remnant of Voldemort's failed curse. Throughout the series, he faces numerous trials, unravels mysteries about his family, and emerges as a symbol of bravery within the wizarding world while striving to thwart Voldemort's resurgence to power.
